# Decisions log

Routine choices taken during autonomous work. When a non-blocking question
comes up mid-task, an autonomous worker makes the reasonable call, logs it
here as one line, and continues — surfacing the log at the end of the task.
This is the rehydration source for "why did it do X?" that isn't in the
agenda (which holds user intent only). Newest first.

`- {date} — {task/item id}: {the choice} — {one-line why}`
